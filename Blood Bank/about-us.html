About Us

Objectives

Objectives for which the State Council is established are as under :

  • ✅ Encouraging Voluntary Blood Donation:
    Promoting non-remunerated blood donation to eliminate reliance on professional donors.
  • ✅ Safe & Efficient Blood Usage:
    Ensuring proper blood utilization through component separation, apheresis, and autologous transfusion.
  • ✅ Training & Skill Development:
    Providing training to technicians, medical officers, and donor motivators in blood center operations.
  • ✅ State & District-Level Blood Centers:
    Establishing and linking nodal centers for efficient blood collection, storage, and distribution.
  • ✅ Quality Assurance & Monitoring:
    Implementing strict quality control measures and monitoring blood centers statewide.
  • ✅ Technology & Database Management:
    Maintaining donor profiles, rare blood group databases, and a centralized communication system.
  • ✅ Financial & Organizational Support:
    Accepting donations, grants, and funding for sustainable operations and employee welfare.

Raktदान 🩸 is a blood donation platform aimed at making blood donation easier and more accessible. We connect blood donors with those in urgent need, promoting the life-saving cause of donating blood. Our goal is to ensure that no patient has to wait for blood, especially in critical situations.
